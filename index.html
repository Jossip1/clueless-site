FAQ

Is this for cheating?

No. Clueless is designed for learning and revision. Do not use it during proctored or closed‑book assessments, and always follow your institution’s academic integrity policy.

Will you store my screenshots?

We prioritize privacy. Your screenshots are processed securely and never sold. You control retention in settings.

What subjects are supported?

Math, sciences, languages, and humanities. We extract text with OCR and guide you through the reasoning with references.

Will this improve my performance?

Yes. Students report faster problem breakdowns and higher practice scores when they focus on AI‑guided explanations first, then reveal answers to check understanding.
